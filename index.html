Infin8 2022

About The Event

IIIT Bangalore is back with a bash with its 7th iteration of INFIN8, better than ever before. Hold on to your seats as we present to you the biggest and the most exciting fest of our institute. We present to you a plethora of events to showcase your inner artist. Battle of Bands, Gaming, Art ,Drama, Singing and Dance competitions and a lot more to experience. Come!! Be a part of this fun ride along with us @IIIT Bangalore. See you at the starting point of a marathon of mind boggling, rib tickling and adrenaline pumping events.

Where

IIIT Bangalore, 26/C, Hosur Rd, Electronics City Phase 1, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560100

When

Friday to Sunday
1st April 2022 to 3rd April 2022
